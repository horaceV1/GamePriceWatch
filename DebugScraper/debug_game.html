      About Crimson Desert

      The world of Pywel is divided as the King of Demeniss lies in a comma. The king is the unifier of all lands, with him incapable at the moment you must step up and be the one to unify them all. The problem is, there are those who would want the power for themselves. Those who felt oppressed would want to break ties, and those who suffered under his rule would only want to stay independent.


      • Explore a vast continent. From the barren Desert to the plains of Akapen, the cold lands of Kwelden and more.
      • Look for hardened mercenaries to join your cause and help you out.
      • Offering a new generation of weapons alongside the traditional ones.
      • Survive the combative mercenaries, deranged rulers, and the mythical beasts.
